How Can We Help?

We've compiled answers to some of our most commonly asked questions below. If you can't find what you're looking for, don't hesitate to contact us directly!

Adoption Questions

What is your adoption process?

Our adoption process involves: 1) Filling out an application, 2) Meeting with an adoption counselor, 3) Meeting the pet, 4) Home check (for some pets), and 5) Finalizing adoption paperwork. The entire process typically takes 1-3 days.

What are the adoption fees?

Adoption fees vary by animal and typically range from $50-$300. This includes spay/neuter surgery, initial vaccinations, microchipping, and a basic health check.

Can I return a pet if it doesn't work out?

Yes, we have a 30-day trial period. If for any reason the adoption isn't working out, you can return the pet and we'll refund your adoption fee. After 30 days, we still accept returns but without the fee refund.

Grooming Services

How often should I groom my pet?

It depends on the breed and coat type. Most dogs benefit from grooming every 4-6 weeks. Cats typically need less frequent grooming, but long-haired breeds may require regular brushing and occasional professional grooming.

Do you groom anxious pets?

Yes, our groomers are experienced with anxious pets. We use gentle techniques and can often accommodate special requests. For extremely anxious pets, we may recommend a consultation first.

Boarding & Daycare

What vaccinations are required for boarding?

We require dogs to be current on Rabies, DHPP, and Bordetella vaccines. Cats need FVRCP and Rabies vaccines. All pets must be flea and tick free.

Can my pets board together?

Yes, pets from the same household can usually board together if they get along well. We have larger suites available for multiple pets.

Products & Supplies

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer local delivery for orders over $50. We also ship nationwide through our online store.

What if my pet doesn't like the food I bought?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee on all food purchases. If your pet doesn't like it, you can return the unused portion within 30 days for a full refund or exchange.
